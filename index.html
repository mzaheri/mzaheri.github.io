I'm a computer science Ph.D candidate at Georgetown University, working with Kobbi Nissim and Adam O'Neill. My main research interest is cryptography and differential privacy. I am particularly interested in the foundations of practical cryptographic protocols and in developing cryptographic protocols with novel properties for emerging applications in databases and networking.
